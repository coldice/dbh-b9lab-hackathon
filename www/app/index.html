ENERTIQ offers the Energy community and their developers a much needed layer to experiment with decentralised applications. Do you want to experiment with a new business model? Tackle inefficiencies? Build a Proof of Concept?

With ENERTIQ, access an open and common layer linking every market participant together. This information layer allows you and others to discover, link, agree and settle with your chosen market participants.
